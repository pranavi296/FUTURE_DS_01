#  Retail Sales Performance Dashboard


##  Overview

This project is an interactive sales performance dashboard built using Power BI to analyze retail transaction data.

The objective was to move beyond basic visualization and focus on extracting meaningful business insights from raw sales data. The dashboard evaluates revenue trends, profitability drivers, regional performance, customer segments, and the impact of discounts on margins.

This project reflects practical data analysis skills relevant to business intelligence and data analytics roles.


##  Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance using KPI-based reporting
* Identify high-performing and low-performing product categories
* Evaluate regional contribution to revenue and profitability
* Measure the impact of discounts on profit margins
* Study monthly sales trends and detect seasonal patterns
* Compare customer segments based on revenue and profit contribution
* Design a clean, executive-level dashboard for decision-making


##  Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Summary

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin (%)
* Average Order Value

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of business health.


### 🔹 Sales Analysis

* Sales by Category and Sub-Category
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Segment-wise Revenue Distribution

Purpose: Identify revenue drivers and growth patterns.


### 🔹 Profitability Analysis

* Profit by Category
* Profit by Region
* Discount vs Profit Relationship

Purpose: Detect areas where high revenue does not translate into strong margins.


### 🔹 Regional Insights

* Sales and Profit comparison across regions
* Geographic performance evaluation

Purpose: Understand territory-level strengths and weaknesses.
